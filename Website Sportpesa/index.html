About Tujiamini

Tujiamini is an initiative by leading online gaming firm SportPesa, managed in partnership with DBA Africa.

We are here to identify, explore and empower the sporting ecosystem with the belief of turning dreams into reality.
With up to 87 winners across 4 categories, rewarding up to KSh3,000,000 worth of products or services each month.

Tujiamini is here to ensure no matter how big or small your sporting dream is, we believe in you.
Simply submit your application and video showcasing your talent and telling us your dream.

Make sure to take note of when your region’s winners will be announced and submit your application ahead of the closing date.
